Grilled Dill Pickle Chicken Thighs

The next time you reach for that last pickle in the jar, hang on to the juice. Chicken marinated in pickle juice makes some mighty tender grilled chicken. The pickle flavor does not overpower, but you need to like the dill pickles to enjoy it

Prep: 5 mins

Cook: 15 mins

Additional: 4 hrs

Total: 4 hrs 20 mins

Servings: 4

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 each skinless chicken thighs
  • 1.5 cups dill pickle juice
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1.5 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 1.5 teaspoon paprika
  • 1.5 teaspoon freeze-dried dill
  • 1.25 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place chicken thighs in gallon-sized resealable bag. Pour pickle juice over the chicken. Seal bag and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Whisk olive oil, seasoned salt, parpika, dill, and pepper together. Remove chicken from pickle juice; discard the juice. Brush all sides of the chicken with the seasoned oil
  4. Grill chicken for 6 minutes. Flip and grill until no longer pink in the center, about 6 minutes more
